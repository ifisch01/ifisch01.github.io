What is Deep Work?

"Deep work is the ability to focus without distraction on a cognitively demanding task. In short, deep work is like a super power in our increasingly competitive twenty-first century economy. And yet, most people have lost the ability to go deep—spending their days instead in a frantic blur of e-mail and social media, not even realizing there’s a better way."

- Cal Newport, Georgetown University

Advantages of deepwork

[ our app, not the psychological concept ]

Publicized Work Schedule

For those that constantly must be in correspondence yet have other projects to work on, our software designates (and publicizes) your convenient email hours, kind of like office hours but for email. Tell people to call you or swing by your desk if it's truly urgent.

Streamlined office tasks

Take care of all administrative tasks at once! Print out that new proposal, make your coffee and swing by IT all in one fell swoop, so as not to interrupt your designated work times.

Time to Yourself

Our app gives you that sweet, sweet alone time, scheduling "blackout hours" where your phone locks out unimportant notifications. Complete with Slack and Google Calendar API integration, our software gets you in the mindset of deep work.
